About us

Established in 1998 and based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we are dedicated to serve the medical industry by our high end medical equipments that include Electro Surgical Generators, Vessel Sealing Systems, many others. Since inception, we are working with an aim to provide advanced and highly sophisticated solutions in the field of surgery by adopting the latest globally accepted technologies. Over the years, we have developed our infrastructural facility to a great extent.

Our company is remarkably growing in medical field with the support of an experienced team of engineers and technocrats. They have decades of experience and expertise in power electronics products, medical equipment, embedded systems, product development and automation systems. We believe in continuous improvisation of our product line and thus, our team makes dedicated efforts in development of new products as well as up-gradations in the existing product line. Our prime motto is to achieve high degree of clinical precision in medical engineering designs keeping the safety of patient on top priority.

We strive for total customer satisfaction and to excel in it, we provide quick and effective after sales support to our clients. In this, we provide complete installation and operational training to clients to get long working life and high performance from the machine. A major aspect in this direction is to keep a strict check on the quality of products.

    Our Objectives Are :

  • Zero customer complaints & delivery failures
  • Providing safe working environment
  • Continual improvement of the processes
  • Improving productivity & cost effectiveness
  • Enhancing competence of the people and internal & external customer satisfaction
  • Developing vendors for critical incoming materials & services
  • Developing robust, innovative & environment friendly processes for existing & new products
  • Reducing waste
  • Conservation of natural resources.
